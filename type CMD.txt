Step 1: Force Your Mobile Browser to Allow the Webcam

Because your phone is connecting over a local IP network link (http://10.72.87.127:8000), modern mobile browsers automatically drop the camera permissions out of caution. We need to flip an internal browser flag to force it to trust this development link.

Type exactly chrome://flags and press Enter.In the search box at the top, type insecure.Look for the setting called "Insecure origins treated as secure".Change its status dropdown from Disabled/Default to Enabled.In the text field right below it, type your exact laptop server IP link:👉 http://10.72.87.127:8000
